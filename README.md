# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The Car Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze and monitor car dealership sales performance. The dashboard provides real-time insights into sales trends, vehicle performance, pricing metrics, and regional sales distribution, enabling data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze Year-to-Date (YTD) and Month-to-Date (MTD) sales performance.
* Monitor Year-over-Year (YOY) growth trends.
* Track average selling prices and cars sold.
* Identify top-performing vehicle categories and regions.
* Provide detailed sales information through interactive visualizations.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Sales Overview

* YTD Total Sales
* MTD Total Sales
* YOY Growth in Total Sales
* Difference Between YTD and Previous YTD Sales

### Average Price Analysis

* YTD Average Price
* MTD Average Price
* YOY Growth in Average Price
* Difference Between YTD and Previous YTD Average Price

### Cars Sold Metrics

* YTD Cars Sold
* MTD Cars Sold
* YOY Growth in Cars Sold
* Difference Between YTD and Previous YTD Cars Sold
